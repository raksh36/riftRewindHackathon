## Video Production Notes

### Technical Requirements
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps or 60fps
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Audio:** Clear narration, background music at -20dB

### Editing Tips
1. Use smooth transitions (fade/dissolve)
2. Zoom in on important UI elements
3. Highlight text as you mention it
4. Keep cursor movements smooth
5. Add subtle background music (lo-fi or electronic)

### Screen Recording Tools
- OBS Studio (free)
- Camtasia (paid)
- Loom (easy, cloud-based)
